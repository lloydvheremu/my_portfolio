About Me

I'm Lloyd B. Vheremu, a Computer Science graduate from the National University of Science and Technology (NUST) in Zimbabwe. With over 2 years of commercial software development experience, I've had the opportunity to work on diverse projects ranging from ERP systems to web applications, building solutions that solve real-world business problems.

My expertise spans full-stack development with a strong foundation in Python and JavaScript. I specialize in building robust backend systems with Django and PostgreSQL, creating modern frontends with Next.js and React, and developing custom ERP solutions using the Odoo framework. I'm passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and delivering high-quality software that makes a difference.

I'm currently seeking remote software developer opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful projects, collaborate with talented teams, and continue growing as an engineer. I'm particularly interested in roles that challenge me to solve complex problems and build scalable, user-focused applications.
